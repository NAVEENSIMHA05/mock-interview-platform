About Our Global Network

MOCK_LINK is a highly reliable marketplace designed to prepare modern freshers for real-world technical interviews. By reducing barriers and providing monetary incentives to active HR managers, we establish a steady stream of mock assessments worldwide.

01

Fresher Entry

Candidates log into their personal portal, configure their target technical streams, upload their active resume file securely, and deposit a standard escrow payment of ₹50.

02

Open Pooling

The system broadcasts the request live into the Open Market Pool. Matching HR professionals can review details and claim requests that align with their background.

03

Google Meet

Upon acceptance, the backend automatically generates a secure Google Meet link. The interview goes live, and actionable feedback/tips are returned directly to the fresher.
